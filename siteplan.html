Site Plan - Motorbiking in Ririe, Idaho

Site Name

The site will be named Ririe Motorbiking Trails.

This name was chosen to clearly convey the website's focus on motorbiking in Ririe, Idaho. It is concise, easy to remember, and clearly identifies the target location.

Optional Domain Availability: bike-ririe.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide motorbiking enthusiasts with a one-stop resource for exploring the best motorbiking trails, scenic routes, and local events in Ririe, Idaho. It will also offer safety tips, weather conditions, and other helpful information for riders of all skill levels.

Scenarios

  • What are the best motorbiking trails for beginners in Ririe, Idaho?
  • Where can I find weather updates for my motorbiking ride in Ririe?
  • Are there any upcoming motorbiking events or meetups in Ririe?

Color Scheme

The color scheme will include:

  • Red: For headings, important accents, and buttons to make the site feel energetic and vibrant. Example: #d32f2f
  • Gray: For background and text elements to provide contrast and legibility. Example: #757575

These colors are chosen for their ability to evoke energy while maintaining readability.

Typography

The website will use the following fonts:

  • Roboto: Used for body text to ensure clarity and readability.
  • Oswald: Used for headings to give the site a bold and modern look.

These fonts were selected for their strong presence and modern appearance, aligning with the theme of adventure and excitement.

Wireframe

Below is the wireframe layout for the website's homepage:

  • Mobile View: A simple vertical layout with the header at the top, followed by sections for the trails, safety tips, weather, and events. The navigation menu will be accessible through a hamburger icon.
  • Desktop View: A horizontal layout with a navigation bar on top, followed by large hero images of the trails, and sections laid out side-by-side for trails, events, and weather information.
